Three animal species have been observed on Velorath during exploration missions. All three are carbon-based life forms but share no structural or behavioral similarities with Earth fauna.

The first species, named the Gralk, is the largest observed creature on Velorath. It is a six-limbed predator approximately 2 meters in length with a tough, ridge-plated exterior. The Gralk moves in short rapid bursts and has demonstrated aggressive behavior toward exploration equipment on two separate occasions. It primarily inhabits the ridge formations and is believed to be territorial. Its diet consists of smaller fauna.

The second species, referred to as Vellin, are small burrowing creatures roughly 20 centimeters long. They travel in groups of 10 to 30 and feed on Duskmoss. They are non-aggressive and retreat underground during storm cycles. Their burrow systems are found extensively across the plains.

The third species, called Shreth, are slow-moving creatures observed near the methane pools. They appear to have a partially aquatic lifestyle adapted to liquid methane rather than water. They have not shown any reaction to human presence and are considered passive. Their feeding behavior has not yet been fully documented.
